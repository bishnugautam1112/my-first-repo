### **UNIT I: INTRODUCTION (Basics)**

**1.1 Management**
*   **Definition:** Getting things done through people efficiently and effectively.
*   **1.1.1 Functions:**
    *   **Planning:** Goal set garne.
    *   **Organizing:** Team ra resource milaune.
    *   **Leading:** Staff lai motivate garne.
    *   **Controlling:** Check garne (Plan vs Actual).
*   **1.1.2 Levels of Management:**
    1.  **Top Level (Strategic):** CEO/Board. Vision banaucha (Long-term).
    2.  **Middle Level (Tactical):** Managers. Department hercha (Medium-term).
    3.  **Lower Level (Operational):** Supervisors. Daily kaam hercha (Short-term).
*   **1.1.3 Principles of Management (Henri Fayol):**
    *   Rules like **Division of Work** (Specialization), **Authority & Responsibility** (Power & Duty), **Unity of Command** (Ek jana boss), **Esprit de Corps** (Team spirit).

**1.2 Organization**
*   **Definition:** A group of people working together for a common goal.
*   **1.2.1 Types:**
    *   **Formal:** Rules, hierarchy, policies huncha (Example: Bank).
    *   **Informal:** Staff bich ko friendship, guff-gaff (Example: Lunch group).
    *   **Virtual:** Physical office hudaina, internet bata kaam huncha (Example: Remote work).

**1.3 Engineering Management**
*   **Concept:** Combining **Technical Knowledge** (Engineering) with **Business Skills** (Management).
*   **1.3.1 Importance in Tech Environment:** Tech chito change huncha, engineers lai manage garna normal manager le sakdaina, tei bhayera EM chahinchha.
*   **1.3.3 Role of Engineering Manager:** Bridge between Tech team and Business goals. Roles: **Interpersonal** (Leader), **Informational** (Monitor), **Decisional** (Resource allocator).

### **UNIT II: PLANNING AND ORGANIZING (Structure)**

**2.1 Planning**
*   **Definition:** Deciding in advance *what* to do and *how* to do it.
*   **2.1.1 Levels:** Matches Mgmt Levels (Strategic, Tactical, Operational).
*   **2.1.2 Steps:** Set Goal $\rightarrow$ Analyze Situation $\rightarrow$ Create Alternatives $\rightarrow$ Select Best Plan $\rightarrow$ Implement.
*   **2.1.3 Tools:**
    *   **SWOT Analysis:** Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats.
    *   **Gantt Chart:** Time schedule bar graph ma dekhaune.
    *   **ICT Tools:** Jira, Trello.
*   **2.1.4 Importance:** Gives direction, reduces risk, reduces waste.

**2.2 Organizing**
*   **Definition:** Arranging resources (manche, paisa, machine) to achieve the plan.
*   **2.2.2 Structure Types (Very Important!):**
    1.  **Line:** Military style. Straight from top to bottom. (Simple but slow).
    2.  **Functional:** Department anusar (IT, HR, Finance). (Expertise huncha, tara silo bancha).
    3.  **Matrix:** **Dui Boss System.** (Functional Manager + Project Manager). Best for complex projects.
    4.  **Network:** Core kaam aafai garne, aru sabai **Outsource** garne. (Flexible & cheap).
    5.  **Hybrid:** Mix of above structures (Used by big companies like Google).

**2.4 Emerging Issues in ICT:**
*   **Issues:** Tech chito purano hune (Obsolescence), Remote work manage garna garho, Talent retention.

### **UNIT III: MOTIVATION AND LEADERSHIP (Psychology)**

**3.1 Motivation**
*   **Definition:** The inner drive that makes people work towards a goal.
*   **3.1.1 Theories:**
    1.  **Maslowâ€™s Hierarchy:** **Pyramid.** (Basic Needs $\rightarrow$ Safety $\rightarrow$ Social $\rightarrow$ Esteem $\rightarrow$ Self-Actualization). Tala ko pura na-bhai mathi jadaina.
    2.  **Herzbergâ€™s Two-Factor:**
        *   **Hygiene Factors:** Salary, AC, Policy. (Yiniharu nabhaye dukha lagcha, tara bhaye pani motivate chai hudaina, just *okay* huncha).
        *   **Motivators:** Achievement, Growth. (Yiniharu le chai sachi nai motivate garcha).
    3.  **Expectancy Theory (Vroom):** **Motivation = E x I x V.** (Effort $\rightarrow$ Performance $\rightarrow$ Reward $\rightarrow$ Value of Reward).
    4.  **Equity Theory:** Staff le aafno input/output arko sathi sanga compare garcha. Fair bhayena bhane demotivate huncha.
*   **3.1.2 Techniques:** Money, Praise, Job Rotation, Participation.

**3.2 Leadership**
*   **Definition:** Influencing others to achieve a goal.
*   **3.2.1 Styles:**
    1.  **Autocratic:** "Maile je bhanyo tehi gar." (Crisis ma ramro, creativity ko lagi naramro).
    2.  **Democratic:** "Sabai milera decision garau." (Slow, but team happy).
    3.  **Servant:** "Ma timiharu ko sewa garchu, timiharu grow hou." (High trust).
    4.  **Transformational:** "Hami duniya change garchau!" (Visionary, inspires innovation).
*   **3.2.2 Learning Organization:** Company jasle galti bata sikcha ra change huna darau-daina.

### **UNIT IV: HRM AND CONTROL (People & Checks)**

**4.1 Human Resource Management (HRM)**
*   **Functions:**
    1.  **Acquisition:** Recruitment (khojne) & Selection (channe).
    2.  **Development:** Training (On-the-job / Off-the-job).
    3.  **Motivation:** Salary & Benefits.
    4.  **Maintenance:** Safety & Relations.
*   **Concepts:**
    *   **Job Analysis:** Job ko postmortem garne (k k chahincha).
    *   **Job Description (JD):** *Duties.* K kaam garne ho?
    *   **Job Specification (JS):** *Skills.* K qualification chahincha?
    *   **Performance Appraisal:** Kaam ko report card. Methods: **MBO** (Goal set garera check garne), **Rating Scale** (Marks dine).

**4.2 Control**
*   **Definition:** Checking if actual performance matches the plan.
*   **4.2.2 Process:** Set Standard $\rightarrow$ Measure Performance $\rightarrow$ Compare $\rightarrow$ Correct Deviation.
*   **4.2.4 ICT Tools for Control:**
    *   **Jira/Asana:** Task track garna.
    *   **MS Project:** Schedule/Budget herna.
    *   **GitHub:** Code control garna.
    *   *Why?* Helps in Real-time tracking, Quality Assurance (QA), and Efficiency.
