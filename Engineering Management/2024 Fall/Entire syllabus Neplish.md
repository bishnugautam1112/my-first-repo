### **UNIT I: INTRODUCTION (Basics)**

**1. Management bhaneko k ho?**
*   **Concept:** Aru manche haru bata kaam liyera goal pura garne kala (art) ho.
*   **Functions (POLC):**
    1.  **Planning:** K garne bhanera sochnu.
    2.  **Organizing:** Team ra saman (resources) milaunu.
    3.  **Leading:** Staff lai "lu gara hai" bhanera motivate garnu.
    4.  **Controlling:** Kaam plan anusar bhayo ki nai check garnu.

**2. Levels of Management (3 Taha):**
1.  **Top Level (Strategic):** CEO, Board. Yiniharu le company ko **Vision** (dur-dristi) banauchan. (Long-term).
2.  **Middle Level (Tactical):** Managers. Department herchan. Top level ko plan lai execute garchan.
3.  **Lower Level (Operational):** Supervisors. Daily kaam herchan.

**3. Engineering Management k ho?**
*   Yo bhaneko **Engineering (Technical)** gyan ra **Management (Business)** gyan ko mix ho.
*   **Engineering Manager:** Yo manche **Tech Team** (Developers) ra **Business Team** (Clients/CEO) bich ko **Pul (Bridge)** ho.

### **UNIT II: PLANNING AND ORGANIZING (Structure)**

**1. Planning (Yojana):**
*   Bhavishya ma k garne bhanera aaja nai decide garnu.
*   **Levels:** Strategic (Thulo plan), Tactical (Department plan), Operational (Daily plan).
*   **Tools:**
    *   **SWOT:** Strength, Weakness, Opportunity, Threat herne.
    *   **Gantt Chart:** Project ko timeline bar graph ma dekhaune.

**2. Organizing (Sanrachana):**
*   **Structure Types (Exam ma sodhcha!):**
    *   **Line:** Military style. Mathi bata tala sidha order aauchha.
    *   **Functional:** Kaam anusar ko department (IT, HR, Finance).
    *   **Matrix (Dui Boss):** Euta staff ko 2 jana boss hunchanâ€”Functional Manager ra Project Manager. Complex project lai best huncha.
    *   **Network:** Aafu main kaam garne, aru sabai bahira (Outsource) garne. (Jastai: Apple le design garcha, China ma banauncha).
    *   **Hybrid:** Mathi ko sabai mix gareko. (Google jasto thulo company le use garcha).

### **UNIT IV: HRM AND CONTROL (Manche ra Check-jaach)**

**1. HRM (Human Resource Management):**
*   Manche (Staff) lai company ma lyaune dekhi nikalne samma ko process.
*   **Functions:**
    *   **Acquisition:** Recruitment (Vacancy kholaune) & Selection (Interview liyera chhanne).
    *   **Training:** Kaam sikaune (On-the-job: Kaam gardai sikne / Off-the-job: Class liyera sikne).
    *   **Performance Appraisal (Report Card):**
        *   *MBO:* Target set garne ani last ma check garne.
        *   *Rating Scale:* 1 to 5 marks dine.
    *   **Reward:** Salary ra Bonus dine.

**2. Control (Niyantran):**
*   Plan anusar kaam bhayo ki nai check garne.
*   **Process:** Standard Set garne $\rightarrow$ Kaam herne $\rightarrow$ Compare garne $\rightarrow$ Galti bhaye sachaune (Corrective Action).
*   **ICT Tools:** **Jira, MS Project, Trello**.
    *   Yiniharu le real-time ma kaam track garna ra efficiency badhauna help garcha.

### **UNIT V: EMERGING TRENDS (Naya Kura)**

**1. Innovation vs. Disruption:**
*   **Innovation:** Naya idea lyaunu (Jastai: iPhone 13 bata 14 aaunu).
*   **Disruption:** Purano market lai purai khaito banaunu (Jastai: **Netflix** aayera CD rental harayo, **Uber** aayera Taxi harayo).

**2. Conflict Resolution (Jhagada Milaune):**
*   Team ma jhagada huda **Collaboration (Win-Win)** technique use garne. Duwai ko kura sunera best solution nikalne.

**3. Recent Tech Trends:**
*   **Agile:** Chito chito software banaune process.
*   **DevOps:** Developer ra IT Operations milera kaam garne.
*   **Cloud/AI:** Aaja bholi ko management ma data ra AI ko use huna thalyo.
